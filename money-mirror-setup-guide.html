Before you start

You'll need two free accounts and two API keys. Don't worry — we'll walk you through getting each one. It takes about 5 minutes.

  • A Google account — you already have this if you use Gmail.
  • A Notion account — free at notion.so. Takes 30 seconds to create.
  • A Claude API key — from Anthropic. We'll show you how to get one below. Costs about $0.01 per month of transactions.
  • A Notion Integration Secret — a private password for your Notion. We'll create it together in Step 2.

What you'll see When you open the copy, you'll see a spreadsheet with a "💰 Money Mirror" menu in the top bar. That's the setup menu.
2

Get your Claude API key

This is the AI brain that reads your bank statements. You pay only for what you use — typically a few cents per month.

Do this
1. Go to console.anthropic.com
2. Create a free account (or log in)
3. Click API Keys in the left menu
4. Click Create Key
5. Copy the key — it starts with sk-ant-...
Tip
Add $5 in credits when prompted. That's enough for thousands of transactions — you likely won't need to add more for months.
🔑
What to copy Your key looks like this: sk-ant-api03-xxxxxxxxxxxx... Keep it private — don't share it with anyone.
3

Create your Notion Integration

This gives Money Mirror permission to write to your Notion — so it can automatically add your transactions to your dashboard.

Do this
1. Go to notion.so/my-integrations
2. Click + New integration
3. Give it any name (e.g. "Money Mirror")
4. Select your workspace from the dropdown
5. Click Save
6. Copy the Internal Integration Secret — it starts with secret_...
Tip
Think of this like a private key to your Notion. Only you have it. Money Mirror uses it to write your transactions, and nothing else.
4

Run the setup wizard

This is the last step. The wizard creates your entire Notion workspace automatically — no manual setup needed.

Do this
1. Open your copy of the Google Sheet
2. Click 💰 Money Mirror in the top menu bar
3. Click 🚀 Run Setup
4. A small window will appear — paste your two keys
5. Click Set Up My Money Mirror
6. Wait about 30 seconds
Important
If Google asks for permission to run the script, click Allow. This is normal — it's asking permission to connect to Notion and Claude on your behalf.
✅
When it's done You'll see a message with a link to your new Notion dashboard. Click it — your Money Mirror is ready.
🪞
You're all set.
Your Money Mirror is running. Every 10 minutes it checks for new bank statements you've uploaded or pasted. Your dashboard updates automatically.
How to use it every month

Once setup is done, using Money Mirror takes about 2 minutes per month. Here's all you do:

A

Download your bank statement

Go to your bank's website or app, download your monthly statement. Any format works: CSV, XLSX, PDF, or even a screenshot.

B

Drop it in your Money Mirror Inbox

Upload the file to the Money Mirror Inbox folder in your Google Drive. That's it — you're done.

Alternatively
You can also copy and paste your transactions directly into the AI Inbox page in Notion. Both methods work.
C

Wait 10 minutes, then check your dashboard

The AI reads your statement, categorizes every transaction, and updates your Notion dashboard automatically. Open Money Mirror in Notion to see your results.

Common questions
Is my financial data safe?
Yes. Your data lives in your own Google account and Notion. We never store or see your bank statements. The AI reads your file, categorizes it, and your data goes directly into your personal Notion — not ours.
How much does the Claude API cost?
Typically $0.01–0.05 per month of transactions. A $5 credit will last most people over a year of normal use.
What banks are supported?
Any bank in the world. As long as you can export a CSV, XLSX, or PDF — or take a screenshot — Money Mirror can read it.
A transaction was categorized incorrectly. What do I do?
Open your Transactions database in Notion and update the Category field manually. It takes 5 seconds. The AI gets it right ~90% of the time.
The setup didn't work. What now?
Check that both API keys are correct and don't have extra spaces. Make sure you clicked "Allow" when Google asked for permission. If it still doesn't work, email us at [YOUR EMAIL] and we'll fix it for you within 24 hours.
